Her Journey — From Dreams to Dedication

“Behind every white coat is a story of dreams, sacrifice, and unbreakable resolve.”

The Childhood Dream

Ever since she was a little girl, Laraib knew she was meant to heal. Inspired by compassion and driven by curiosity, she dreamed of wearing a white coat and serving humanity. Her journey into medicine began with a simple wish — to make lives better.

The First Step

Years of dedication and hard work led her to her first major milestone — appearing in the MDCAT exam. On her very first attempt, she gave it her all… but despite her best efforts, she couldn’t secure admission into a government medical college.

It was a heartbreaking moment — the kind that tests your dreams and shakes your spirit.

Solace in Faith

In the quiet moments after her first attempt, when the world felt heavy and dreams seemed distant, she found solace in these sacred words. This ayat echoed in her heart, reminding her that patience, faith, and resilience are never wasted. With every verse, she gathered hope and strength to rise again — and today, her journey stands as a testament to unwavering determination and the power of trust in Allah's plan.

“Ya Musabbib al-Asbab, ya Mufattih al-Abwab, iftah lana bab al-khayr, ya Rabb al-Alamin. Meri madad farma.”

“O Causer of causes, O Opener of doors, open for us the door of goodness, O Lord of the worlds. Help me.”

Rising Stronger

But Laraib’s story is not one of giving up. It’s a story of resilience. Instead of backing down, she gathered her strength. She studied harder, pushed her limits, and prepared once again to chase her dream.

The Triumph

In her second attempt at MDCAT, Laraib didn’t just pass — she soared. She secured 194 marks out of 200, a remarkable achievement that opened the doors she had long dreamed of.

Today

Today, Laraib stands as a doctor. A living example of what happens when hard work meets unshakable determination. Her journey inspires countless others to believe — that with passion and perseverance, dreams do come true.

“This journey is not just mine — it belongs to every dreamer who dares to try one more time.” — Dr. Laraib
